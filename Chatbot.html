Chatbot Interface

This chatbot was designed for a financial institution to assist with setting up businesses. The interface was designed for desktop, and utilised the chatbot functions of quick replies and buttons. A side panel was implemented into the interface to meet the user demand of easily accessible input changes and to enable a place to store and integrate editable forms into the chat.
